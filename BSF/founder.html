Become a Founder


First founders gave us the symposium.

Legacy founders ensure it endures and shapes the future.


Who are Legacy Founders?


Philanthropists who care about exchange of ideas, public access to science and diversity in leadership.

Dermatologists curious about the skin and how knowledge will lead to breakthrough treatments.

Skin researchers unlocking secrets of skin structure and function in health and disease.

Departments of Dermatology embracing the Montagna Symposium as part of resident training and lifelong learning to ensure that clinically relevant questions get answered.

Corporations springing from and acting in the skin niche.

Foundations dedicated to discovering cures and pathways to quality of life.

Pioneers like Montagna inspirited to viably capture the diversity of skin across humans and endangered non-human primates.


How can I become a founder?


Fulfill pledges by 2021, the 70th anniversary year of the Symposium on the Biology of Skin.


Individuals

Fulfill pledges of $30,000 or more by 2021. Be acknowledged forever as a Founder, in order of amount and date of pledge.

Corporations

Fulfill pledges of $40,000 or more by 2021. Be acknowledged for 9 years in order of amount and date of pledge. Renewable as Founders forever.

Foundations

Fulfill pledges of $30,000 or more by 2021. Be acknowledged as a Founder for 9 years in order of amount and date of pledge. Renewable as Founders forever.

Donate

Give any gift, and be acknowledged through 2021. Individuals will have the opportunity to become a Founder based upon their lifetime contributions or contributions in their honor.
